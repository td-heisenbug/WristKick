WristKick Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 16, 2025

Our Commitment

WristKick values your privacy as much as your next goal. We don’t collect, store, or share any personal data—ever. The game runs entirely on your Apple Watch.

What We Collect

Nothing. WristKick does not gather personal information, gameplay data, or analytics of any kind. All gameplay and progress stay on your device.

Local Game Data

Small bits of data like your chosen country or match progress are stored locally on your Apple Watch using Apple’s secure system storage. This never leaves your device or syncs online.

No Tracking, No Ads

WristKick is completely ad-free and does not use any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We don’t use Firebase, Crashlytics, or Google Analytics. Your play sessions are yours alone.

Offline Game

WristKick is designed to work fully offline. You can play anytime, anywhere—no internet connection required.

Support & Contact

If you have any questions or feedback, reach out to tdheisenbug@gmail.com. We’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
